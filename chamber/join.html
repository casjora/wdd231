NP (Non-Profit) Membership

Cost: No Fee

  • Listing in the online chamber directory.
  • Access to monthly newsletters.
  • Invitation to non-profit-specific workshops.
  • Networking opportunities at general events.
×

Bronze Membership

Cost: $100 / year

  • All NP benefits.
  • One free admission to a "Business After Hours" event.
  • Discounted rates for other chamber events.
  • Business name listed on the chamber website.
×

Silver Membership

Cost: $250 / year

  • All Bronze benefits.
  • Two spotlight features on the chamber's social media per year.
  • Enhanced directory listing with logo.
  • One dedicated email blast to chamber members.
×

Gold Membership

Cost: $500 / year

  • All Silver benefits.
  • Prominent logo placement on the chamber homepage.
  • Free vendor booth at the annual chamber expo.
  • Priority invitations to exclusive, high-profile events.
