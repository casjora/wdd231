Tegucigalpa, Honduras

Honduras is a Central American nation known for its diverse landscapes, ranging from rainforests to coral reefs. It is home to a rich cultural heritage, with influences from indigenous Lenca people and Spanish colonization. Honduras is renowned for its Mayan ruins at Copán, a significant archaeological site. The country is also known for its coffee production and the vibrant coral reefs of the Bay Islands, a popular destination for diving and snorkeling.
