OPI - Over The Phone Interpretation

Over-the-phone interpretation is in high demand nowadays.

We understand how difficult your job is and how little room for error is accepted from you.

That's why we've created this toolbox so you know where to find the assistance you need.

What's in here for me?

Have you always wondered:

  • What should I do if another party joins the call?
  • What should I do if a client needs to leave the room?
  • What's the process I need to follow in case I encounter an unfamiliar term during a call?

That's why you'll find here a guide of industry standardized protocols (how to handle day-to-day scenarios), and the most accepted terminology map based on the industry of your call!
