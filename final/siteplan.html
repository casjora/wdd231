Site Plan - Interpreter Toolbox

   
   
       
           

Site Name:

           

Interpreter Toolbox

           

                Reasoning: I chose this name as it implies the purpose of the site, which is to provide a collection of useful tools and resources for interpreters.            

           

Optional domain availability: interpreter-toolbox.com (Available)

       
       
           

Site Purpose:

           

                The "Interpreter Toolbox" aims to become a learning hub and an in-call "go-to" tool to aid over-the-phone interpreters in their daily work. It will provide quick access to standardized protocols for handling various call scenarios and offer terminology guides for different industries to ensure accuracy and professionalism.            

       
       
           

Scenarios:

           

                This site will address common questions and scenarios faced by interpreters, such as:            

           
                   
  • I need to find the best term for a word in a medical setting. Where can I find the one accepted in the industry?
    •                
  • How do I handle a call where both the English and the non-English speaker are talking at the same time?
    •
